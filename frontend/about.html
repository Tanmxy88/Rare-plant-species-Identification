About PlantConserve

Preserving botanical diversity through technology and community.

Our Mission

PlantConserve uses AI to identify and catalog rare and endangered plant species, helping support conservation efforts and biodiversity monitoring.

Project Overview

Our hybrid model combines deep learning and morphological analysis to classify plants using image features like leaf contours and venation. Originally designed for desktop use, the system can be upgraded for mobile and remote field environments, enabling real-time, offline plant identification for researchers and citizen scientists.

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) for feature extraction
  • K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) for classification
  • Transfer learning and data augmentation for robustness
