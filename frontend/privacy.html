Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

  • We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • Uploaded images are used only for plant identification and deleted after processing, unless you choose to contribute them for research.
  • We use cookies only to improve your experience; we do not track personal behavior or sell your data.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at support@plantconserve.org.
  • Our website may contain links to external sites; we are not responsible for their privacy practices.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • We may update this policy; continued use of the site means you accept any changes.
